## How It Works

1. Buy $XBRDG on [pump.fun](https://pump.fun/coin/6vUhppYep18WSncUDR6Brt9yZw31ycLDPDEHo13pump) or any Solana DEX
2. Go to [xbridgemcp.com/#loyalty](https://xbridgemcp.com/#loyalty)
3. Enter your Solana wallet address
4. If you hold enough, you get a discount code or upgraded limits

Your wallet balance is checked on-chain. No staking required — just hold.
